Lasagna Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook beef in a skillet until browned. Drain fat.
  3. Add sauce and water to beef, simmer.
  4. Mix cheeses: Combine ricotta, 1 cup mozzarella, Parmesan, and egg. Season.
  5. Assemble: Layer meat sauce, noodles, and cheese mixture in a 9x13 dish. Repeat layers, ending with noodles and sauce. Top with 2 cups mozzarella.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes, covered with foil. Remove foil and bake 15 more minutes. Let rest before serving.
